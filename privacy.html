GoldenEar Privacy Policy

The Company collects and uses personal information as follows for the provision of the GoldenEar Service.

  • Internal user identifier (UUID)

Article 4 (Right to Refuse Consent)

  • The Company shall destroy personal information without delay when such information becomes unnecessary due to the expiration of the retention period or the achievement of the processing purpose.
  • Upon account withdrawal, personal information shall be immediately destroyed. Information required to be retained under applicable laws and regulations shall be retained for the required period and then destroyed.

Article 6 (Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties)

  • The Company does not provide users' personal information to external parties.

Article 7 (Entrustment of Personal Information Processing)

Article 8 (Rights of Users)
